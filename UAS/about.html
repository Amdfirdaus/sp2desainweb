About Us

Rooted in craftsmanship, inspired by simplicity — we build timeless furniture that brings warmth to your space.

Our Story

Started in a small wood workshop in 2018, our journey began with a simple goal — to create furniture that blends comfort, beauty, and durability. Each piece we make is carefully designed and built by hand, using locally sourced materials and traditional techniques.

Our Journey

From a small workshop to a growing furniture brand, here's how it all started.

2018 — Humble Beginnings

Started as a small wood workshop in Banyuwangi with a passion for handmade furniture.

2020 — First Product Line

Launched our first furniture collection using 100% local materials.

2023 — Custom Design Service

Opened up for custom furniture requests and expanded our team of artisans.

2025 — Opened First Showroom

Our first physical showroom opened to the public, offering a full design experience.
